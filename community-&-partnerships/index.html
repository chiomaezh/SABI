Service Overview S A B I

Community & Partnerships

Strategy for discoverability and cultural currency. Analysing your brand identity, market segment, and short term goals to curate a bespoke dossier of 50 industry contacts to platform and endorse your brand. Looking beyond the biggest names, and redirecting you to more niche, specialised leads. This service follows a fixed structure and rate.

Our Approach

1 — Brand Positioning

Mapping your brand alongside 15 direct competitor brands. Identifying the cultural cues and associations that shape your brand identity.

2 — Discoverability

Defining the profile of your target customer or client. Notes on visibility, narrative consistency, community signals, and market reach across all acquisition channels.

3 — Whitespace

Reviewing your short-term business objectives. Scouting untapped market opportunities, unmet customer or client needs, and potential to differentiate from competitors.

4 — Strategic Alignment

Identifying 15 complementary brands — indirect competitors or peers who share your target audience — for collaborative projects and shared initiatives that boost cultural relevance.

5 — Referrals

A dossier of 50 industry contacts curated to support the growth, visibility, and cultural integration of your brand. Leads are drawn from across the following categories, according to your business's priorities:

  • Press & Media
  • Sales & Merchandising
  • Concierge Services & Booking Platforms
  • Members' Clubs & Community Spaces
  • Influencer Partnerships
  • VIP & Guest Network
  • PR & Talent Management
  • Awards & Accreditations
